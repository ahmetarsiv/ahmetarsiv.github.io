About Me.

Ahmet Arşiv, also known as Ahmet Sefa Arşiv (born November 24, 2002, Istanbul, Republic of Turkey), is a programmer, designer, technology entrepreneur, and philanthropist. Besides being born in the Republic of Turkey, he does not hold any other citizenship. Ahmet Sefa Arşiv is also currently an open-source software developer.

Ahmet Sefa Arşiv, actively participating in web programming and online applications, embarked on a major project and developed the first open-source e-Examproject in Türkiye and globally. The aim of the developed project is to enable small or large-scale educational institutions to transition to the digital world, thereby reducing workforce and costs. The coding languages used include the Laravel Framework(PHP) and Next.js(React.js).

In his early years, Arşiv, who is from the Fatih district of Istanbul, was born on November 24, 2002, in Istanbul, where his father worked as a printer. He has Greek origins on hisfather's side. After completing his primary education in Istanbul, he finished his secondary education at Karaman Temizel-Ünlü Vocational and Technical Anatolian High School(formerly known as Computer High School).

Arşiv, who entered the business world at an early age, started with small-scale entrepreneurship in the 2010s. In 2019, he received WordPress training in Braga, Portugal, to enhance his software skills.
